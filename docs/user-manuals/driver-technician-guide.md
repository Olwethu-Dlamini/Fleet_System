# FleetScheduler Pro — Driver and Technician User Manual

**Role:** Driver / Technician
**Version:** 1.0
**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## Introduction

This manual explains how to use the FleetScheduler Pro mobile app as a driver or technician. The app shows you the jobs assigned to you, lets you update job status, request extra time when you need it, and manages GPS tracking in a way that respects your privacy.

You do not need any technical knowledge to use this guide. Each section walks through the steps clearly.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open the FleetScheduler Pro app on your mobile device.
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Tap **Log In**.

### GPS Consent Screen

The first time you log in, you will see a **GPS Consent** screen before you reach the main app. This screen explains what location data is collected and why.

**Why GPS consent is required:**
- FleetScheduler Pro tracks your location during working hours to help schedulers direct jobs to the nearest available driver.
- Your location is only shared during **active job periods** — not when you are off the clock.
- This is required by privacy law (POPIA and GDPR) to protect your personal data.

**What to do on the consent screen:**
1. Read the information about what data is collected.
2. Tap **I Agree** to allow GPS tracking, or **Decline** if you do not consent.
3. If you agree, you proceed to the main app.

> **Note:** If you decline GPS consent, your location will not appear on the scheduler's map. A record of your decision is stored for legal compliance. You can change your consent decision later — ask your administrator or scheduler.

### Navigating the App

After logging in, you will see a simplified navigation bar with **3 tabs**:

| Tab | What It Contains |
|-----|-----------------|
| Dashboard | Overview of your jobs for today |
| My Jobs | Full list of jobs assigned to you |
| Notifications | Your notification history |

## Viewing Assigned Jobs

You can only see jobs that have been assigned to you by a scheduler or administrator.

### Viewing Your Job List

1. Tap the **My Jobs** tab.
2. All jobs currently assigned to you are listed, sorted by scheduled date and time.
3. Tap on any job to open the full job details.

### What Job Details Show You

When you open a job, you will see:
- **Customer name** and **contact phone number**.
- **Job address** — shown on a map so you can see exactly where to go.
- **Scheduled date and time** — when you are expected to arrive.
- **Job type** — the category of work (e.g., HVAC service, plumbing repair).
- **Priority** — low, medium, or high.
- **Description** — any instructions or notes from the scheduler.
- **Directions button** — tap to get turn-by-turn directions and estimated travel time to the job site.

## Updating Job Status

Job status tells your scheduler and manager how the job is progressing. You are responsible for keeping your job status up to date.

### How Job Status Changes

Jobs move through the following stages:

| Status | What It Means | Who Changes It |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **Pending** | Job created, waiting for assignment | System / Admin |
| **Assigned** | You have been assigned | Admin / Scheduler |
| **In Progress** | Job has started | Automatic (when scheduled start time arrives) |
| **Completed** | Job is finished | You confirm it manually |
| **Cancelled** | Job was cancelled | Admin / Scheduler |

> **Note:** Jobs automatically move to **In Progress** when the scheduled start time arrives. You do not need to manually start a job.

### Completing a Job

When you have finished the work at a job site:

1. Open the job from the **My Jobs** tab.
2. Tap the **Complete Job** button.
3. A confirmation dialog appears asking: "Are you sure you want to mark this job as complete?"
4. Tap **Confirm**.
5. The app records your GPS location at the moment of completion (if you have granted GPS consent). This creates an audit trail showing where you were when the job was completed.
6. The job status changes to **Completed**.

> **Important:** You cannot undo a job completion. Make sure the work is genuinely finished before tapping Complete.

## Requesting Time Extensions

Sometimes a job takes longer than expected. FleetScheduler Pro lets you request extra time from your scheduler without needing to call or message separately.

### When You Can Request Extra Time

The **Add More Time** button only appears on jobs that:
- Are currently **In Progress**.
- Are **assigned to you**.

You cannot request extra time on jobs that are pending, completed, or cancelled.

### How to Request Extra Time

1. Open the in-progress job from the **My Jobs** tab.
2. Tap the **Add More Time** button.
3. Fill in the time extension request form:
   - **Extra Time Needed** — How much additional time you need (in minutes or hours).
   - **Reason** — A brief explanation of why you need more time (e.g., "more complex repair than expected", "waiting for customer to unlock access", "traffic delay on return").
4. Tap **Submit Request**.
5. Your scheduler or administrator receives an instant notification about your request.

### Tracking Your Request

After submitting:
- Your request is marked as **Pending** while your scheduler reviews it.
- You will receive a push notification when the request is **Approved** or **Denied**.
- If approved, your additional time is noted on the job.
- If denied, the original end time still applies.

> **Note:** Only one active time extension request is allowed per job at a time. You cannot submit a second request until the first one is resolved.

## Notifications

FleetScheduler Pro sends you alerts to keep you informed about your job assignments and any changes.

### The Notification Bell

- A **bell icon** appears in the top-right corner of the screen.
- A **badge number** on the bell shows how many unread notifications you have.

### Opening Notifications

1. Tap the **Notifications** tab or the **bell icon**.
2. Your notification history is listed, with unread items highlighted.
3. Tap a notification to go to the relevant job.
4. Tap **Mark All as Read** to clear the badge.

### Notification Types You Will Receive

| Type | When You See It |
|------|----------------|
| **Job Assigned** | A new job has been assigned to you |
| **Schedule Change** | The date, time, or address of your assigned job has changed |
| **Time Extension Approved** | Your extra time request was approved |
| **Time Extension Denied** | Your extra time request was denied |

### Managing Notification Preferences

1. Open your profile or notification settings.
2. Toggle **Email Notifications** on or off.
3. When enabled, important alerts are also sent to your registered email address.

## GPS Tracking

This section explains what GPS tracking means for you and how to manage it.

### What Data Is Collected

- FleetScheduler Pro records your **current location** at regular intervals.
- Tracking only happens **during active job hours** — not when you are off duty.
- Your location is used to:
  - Help schedulers assign the nearest driver to incoming jobs.
  - Record your location when you mark a job as completed (audit trail).

### GPS Consent — What Happens When You Agree

- Your location is shared with your scheduler and administrator during working hours.
- Location data is stored securely and used only for fleet management purposes.

### GPS Consent — What Happens When You Decline

- Your location will not appear on the scheduler's live tracking map.
- A record of your decline is stored for legal compliance (POPIA/GDPR requirement).
- You can still use all other app features normally.

### How to Grant or Review GPS Consent

- If you agreed to GPS during first login, tracking is active automatically when you are on shift.
- If you declined and want to change your decision, contact your administrator or scheduler to update your consent status.

### When GPS Is Active

GPS tracking runs while:
1. You are logged into the app.
2. The app has permission to access your device's location (you granted this in your phone's settings).
3. Your GPS consent is set to "agreed".

> **Note:** If your phone's location permission for FleetScheduler Pro is set to "Never", tracking will not work even if you have agreed in the app. Go to your phone's **Settings > Apps > FleetScheduler Pro > Permissions > Location** and set it to **While Using App** or **Always**.

## Troubleshooting

### GPS Not Working

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| My location is not showing on the map | Check that you agreed to GPS consent in the app, and that Location permission is granted on your phone |
| GPS is working but showing wrong location | Move outside or to a more open area for a better signal. Restart the app if needed |
| I want to change my GPS consent decision | Contact your scheduler or administrator |

### Notifications Not Arriving

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I am not getting push notifications | Check that notification permission is granted for FleetScheduler Pro in your phone's settings |
| I am not getting email notifications | Check that email notifications are toggled on in your notification settings |
| Notifications are delayed | This can happen on slower connections. Check your internet connection |

### Cannot See Assigned Jobs

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| My Jobs tab shows no jobs | You may not have any jobs scheduled yet. Check with your scheduler |
| I know I am assigned but the job is not showing | Try pulling down to refresh the jobs list, or log out and log back in |

### Cannot Complete a Job

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Complete Job button is not showing | The job may not be In Progress yet, or you may not be the assigned driver/technician |
| App crashes when completing a job | Ensure you have internet connection. Try again after reconnecting |

### Cannot Request Time Extension

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Add More Time button is not visible | The job must be In Progress and assigned to you. Check the job status |
| Request was denied and I need more time | Contact your scheduler directly |

## Quick Reference

### Job Status Progression

```
Pending → Assigned → In Progress (automatic) → Completed (you confirm)
```

### Things You Can Do

- View jobs assigned to you
- Open job details (customer info, address, map, directions)
- Mark a job as completed
- Request a time extension on in-progress jobs
- View and manage notifications
- Manage GPS consent and notification preferences

### Things You Cannot Do

- Create or edit jobs
- Assign drivers or vehicles
- View other drivers' jobs
- Access reports or settings
- Add or manage users or vehicles
